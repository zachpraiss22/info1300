IS THE CORNELL CLASS OF 2016?



The Senior class of Cornell University is a diverse group. We came into this University with:

    • 3,270 people strong
      52.7% Women
      66% attended public high schools
      20.5% identify as under-represented minorities
      and 91% were in the top 10% of their class

    • The class of 2016 comes from Argentina, Canada, Ethopia, Spain, Indonesia, Lebanon, Taiwan, Sri Lanka, Chile, Sweden, Islamic State of Iran, Malaysia, El Salvador, Australia, Paraguay, France, China, Hong Kong, Pakistan, Egypt, Greece, India, and many, many more places.

WHERE

CAN I DONATE TO IN THE SCC?



Through the Senior Class Campaign (SCC), you can choose where you want to donate within the university. You can donate to the Cornell Annual Fund or you can choose the specific aspect of Cornell that you would like to see your donation go towards.


The SCC is your opportunity to give back to the university, programs, or clubs that have been formative in your college experience here at Cornell.



Cornell Annual Fund

The Cornell Annual Fund helps finance much of the programs and departments that make Cornell what it is. It helps support the students, staff, as well as the continuous growth of the University. The Cornell Annual Fund works to keep Ezra Cornell's mission alive.

Your Choice

You can choose to give to a specific part of Cornell. This can be your college, a club/organization that you were part of, or any other aspect of Cornell that you feel would benefit the most from your donation.
